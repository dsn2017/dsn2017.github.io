Please note that, for each paper in the main track, at least one paid full registration (i. e., Conference member, Conference non-member, All access member, or All access non-member) is required. Registration should be done before April 21, 2017 to include the accepted paper in the proceedings. "Member" refers to IEEE (your IEEE affiliation number is required).

Conference Only Registration in all categories includes attendance at all DSN sessions on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, breaks and lunches on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, Monday welcome reception, banquet, and proceedings of the conference on a USB stick.

Workshop/Tutorial Only Registration in all categories give access to the tutorials and workshops on Monday, breaks and lunch on Monday, welcome reception, and proceedings of the conference on a USB stick.

All Access Registration provides access to all workshop and tutorials on Monday, DSN sessions on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, breaks and lunches on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday, welcome reception on Monday, banquet, and proceedings of the conference on a USB stick.

Students are required to provide proof of full-time student status (such as a valid student ID) at the time of registration.

Cancellations are subject to a $100 processing fee and must be received by June 15, 2017. There will be no refunds after June 15, 2017.
